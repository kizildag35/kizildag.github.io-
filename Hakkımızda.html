About Umbrella Corporation


The Umbrella Corporation is an omnipresent corporation founded by Dr. Alexander Roland Isaacs and Dr. James Marcus, after Dr. Marcus successfully developed the T-Virus, a groundbreaking viral medical treatment, to cure his daughter Alicia's Progeria. Our state of the art biomedical engineering labs and research facilities focus on maximizing health, happiness and the advancement of the human race. In other words, our business is life itself!
